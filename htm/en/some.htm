Cloud hub

Depending on your deployment, you may arrive at a cloud hub when you start . In a cloud hub, you can view and interact with apps. However, you cannot create new apps, or develop apps further.

In a cloud hub, apps can be organized in collections. There are public collections and private collections.

Your organization creates public collections and shares those collections with you. These collections contain apps that have been developed and shared by your organization.

You can create private collections. To add an app to a private collection, click the plus icon on the app. In the window that opens, you can also remove the app from a collection, or create a new collection.

Another way to create collections is to click your profile icon, and then select my My collections. In the window that opens, you can also view and open collections.

You can also favorite apps. To favorite an app, click the star icon on the app. To view your favorites, click your profile icon, and then select my My favorites.
